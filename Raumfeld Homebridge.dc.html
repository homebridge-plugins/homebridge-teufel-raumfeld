homebridge-raumfeld

Raumfeld, in Apple Home.

Control every Teufel Raumfeld zone from HomeKit and AirPlay to them from any iPhone — no extra hub, no rebuy.

$npm i -g homebridge-raumfeld

AirPlay 2 streaming
Pick a zone from Control Center and stream any app straight to it.

Multiroom groups
Grouped rooms appear as one accessory you can trigger in scenes.

Per-zone volume
